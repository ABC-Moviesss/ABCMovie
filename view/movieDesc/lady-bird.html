Lady Bird

IMDb rating 7.4/10

Christine McPherson is a senior at the Immaculate Heart of Mary Catholic High School in California. She gives herself the nickname "Lady Bird". Despite her family's financial struggles, she longs to attend a prestigious college. Her mother, Marion, does not believe Christine's dreams are possible and tells her that she is ungrateful for what she has.
